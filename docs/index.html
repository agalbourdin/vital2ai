90+ Metrics

From steps and heart rate to nutrition and blood pressure. Everything HealthKit has to offer.

AI-Ready Format

CSVs are structured specifically for LLMs. No cleaning required—just insights.

Workout Depth

Detailed workout summaries including heart rate zones, elevation, and metabolic data.

Privacy First

On-device processing. No accounts. No tracking. Your data never leaves your iPhone.

Biometric Security

Protect your exports with FaceID or TouchID before they are even generated.

Auto-Cleanup

Exported files are automatically deleted after sharing for maximum security.
